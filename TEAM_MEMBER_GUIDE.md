# Team Member Quick Guide

## Welcome to the Onboarding Tracker!

This guide will help you log your onboarding sessions quickly and easily.

## Logging In

1. Go to the Onboarding Tracker website
2. Enter your email address (provided by your manager)
3. Enter your password (provided by your manager)
4. Click "Sign In"

## Logging an Onboarding Session

Once you're logged in, you'll see a simple form:

### Step 1: Enter Client Name
Type the name of the client you did the onboarding session with.

### Step 2: Enter Account Number
Type the account number for this client.

### Step 3: Select Date
- The date defaults to today
- Click the date field to change it if needed
- Select the date when the onboarding session actually happened

### Step 4: Submit
Click the "Log Session" button to save your entry.

## What Happens Next?

- Your submission is saved instantly
- It appears in your "Recent Sessions" list below the form
- Your manager can see it in the master calendar
- The session status starts as "pending" and may be updated by your manager

## Viewing Your Sessions

Below the submission form, you'll see your 10 most recent sessions, showing:
- Client name
- Account number
- Session number (automatically tracked)
- Date of the session
- Current status (pending, completed, etc.)

## Session Status Meanings

- **Pending**: Session logged, waiting for review
- **Completed**: Session confirmed as completed
- **Cancelled**: Session was cancelled
- **Rescheduled**: Session was moved to a different date
- **No Show**: Client didn't attend

Note: You can log sessions, but only your manager can update the status.

## Tips

✅ **Log sessions as soon as possible** after they happen
✅ **Double-check the account number** before submitting
✅ **Use the correct date** - especially if logging past sessions
✅ **Check your recent sessions** to verify your entries

## Troubleshooting

### Can't Log In?
- Make sure you're using the correct email and password
- Check for typos
- Contact your manager if you forgot your password

### Submission Failed?
- Check your internet connection
- Make sure all fields are filled out
- Try refreshing the page and logging in again

### Don't See Your Session?
- It should appear immediately in your recent sessions
- Scroll down to check the list
- Refresh the page if needed

## Signing Out

When you're done:
1. Click the "Sign Out" button in the top right corner
2. You'll be returned to the login page

## Need Help?

Contact your manager if you:
- Can't log in
- Have questions about which sessions to log
- Need your password reset
- Notice any errors in your submissions
